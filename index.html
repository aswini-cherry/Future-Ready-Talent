Breakfast

The first meal of the day especially when taken in the morning.It replenishes your supply of glucose to boost your energy levels and alertness, while also providing other essential nutrients required for good health

Lunch

A light midday meal between breakfast and dinner.It raises your blood sugar level in the middle of the day, which gives you the energy you need for the rest of the day.

Dinner

The main meal of the day, eaten in the evening or at midday.Having a healthy dinner is linked to good sleep, greater resilience to stress, better digestion, stable blood sugar and lower anxiety.

Supper food

A meal eaten in the evening, esp. Supper is used especially when the meal is an informal one eaten at home, while dinner tends to be the term chosen when the meal is more formal.

Pasta ravioli

Ravioli is a type of pasta that is shaped into small squares, filled with cheese and served in a sauce. Ravioli is a traditional Italian pasta dish and often prepared with loved ones for the feast table at Christmastime or for a family celebration.
